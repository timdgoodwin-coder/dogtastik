Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how DogTastik ("DogTastik," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This policy is written to comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018. For the purposes of these laws, DogTastik is the data controller of your personal information. You can contact us at dogtastik@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.

Order & Dog Details

When you place an order, we collect information about your dog (name, breed, personality traits, favorite things, preferred musical style, and similar details), and any photos or lyric preferences you choose to submit, so we can compose your Jingle.

Payment Information

Payments are processed by our third-party payment processor, Stripe. We do not collect or store your full card number, expiration date, or CVC on our servers — Stripe handles this directly in accordance with its own privacy policy and PCI-DSS compliance standards.

Usage & Device Information

Like most websites, we may automatically collect certain technical information, such as your browser type, device information, and pages visited, to help us operate and improve the Service.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to keep you signed in and to remember your order progress as you move through the site. This is functional, not advertising-related, and stays on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and deliver your custom Jingle
  • To process payments and send order confirmations or status updates
  • To respond to customer support requests
  • To maintain and improve the Service
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

4. Our Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we rely on the following legal bases to process your personal information:

  • Contract — to create your account, take your order, and deliver your Jingle
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the Service secure, prevent fraud, and improve what we offer
  • Consent — where you have separately agreed, such as using your dog's Jingle as a promotional sample
  • Legal obligation — where we must retain or disclose information to comply with the law

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the third-party service providers necessary to operate the Service, including:

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — website hosting

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of DogTastik, our customers, or others. Some of our service providers, including Stripe and Vercel, may process or store data outside the UK. Where this happens, we rely on appropriate safeguards recognized under UK GDPR, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or the provider's own certified compliance mechanisms.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and order information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request erasure of your information ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Receive your information in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent, such as use of your dog's Jingle as a promotional sample (see our Terms & Conditions)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at dogtastik@gmail.com. If you're not satisfied with how we've handled your information, you have the right to complain to the UK's data protection regulator, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), at ico.org.uk.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent changes. Material changes will be communicated where appropriate.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, email us at dogtastik@gmail.com.
